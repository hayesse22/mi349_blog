Unfortunately, if you are a Celtics fan, the season did not end the way you wish it did. The Celtics fell short to the New York Knicks, who just recently lost to Indiana in the Conference Finals. Also, Tatum had injured himself in the last game of their season, who likely won't see the court all of next year. Even with this wave of "bad luck", the Celtics still have a great chance to keep their team together, or if they wanted to, could easily trade players away due to how much good talent is on the team all together. There's speculation going around that Jaylen Brown might get traded, who if traded, would bring back a lot of talent in return. However, this likely would upset many fans as he's been on the team the second longest. In my opinion, I think the Celtics should keep the team they currently have constructed, due to just recently winning the NBA Finals just last season. There's an issue though, multiple players on the team make tens of millions of dollars a year, this makes it tough on the organization to pay all the salaries at once. A trade is likely going to occur due to that, I just hope the team makes good decisions if they do decide to.
